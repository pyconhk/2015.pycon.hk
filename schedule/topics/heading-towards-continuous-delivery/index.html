Talk: Heading towards Continuous Delivery

Speaker: Steven Mak

Company: Odd-e

Language: English (with English Slides)

Intermediate

About Speaker

Steven Mak is an Agile Coach of Odd-e team. He is interested in every part of software development activities and a variety of programming languages, from mainstream to the very exotic. He considers code quality very important as this is what fundamentally the programmers are working on and also what software company is delivering. At the moment, He focused on extremely self-managing organisations, security coding and testing, and product idea validation and experimentation.

He begins interested in programming while he was in primary school. Later obtained a Bachelor degree in Computer Science at the University of Hong Kong. To pursue better understanding of teams, customers, and products, he completed a Master degree in Business Administration at Imperial College London.

About the Topic

In this session, Steven introduces the idea of continuous delivery and how companies can get towards continuous delivery with cases. He will also give out some ideas you can try it out in your own organization.
