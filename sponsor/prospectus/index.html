Sponsor Prospectus

For 2015, we are going to organize first PyCON in Hong Kong with the date on November 7 (Saturday) after we visited PyCON APAC a couple times. We will have keynotes, talks and workdshops. We are expecting about 150 participants.

In becoming a sponsor, we are offering to you:

  • Matchmaking opportunities for companies with local enterprises;
  • Marketing opportunities to be featured in conference materials and presented in media announcements;
  • Networking with highly capable IT professionals

The conference has proven to attract participants usually scattered in their own different groups. By becoming a sponsor, your organisation will be able to build relationships with them. Please refer to our event web site at http://2015.pycon.hk/ .
